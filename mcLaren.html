The new McLaren 600LT.

​ McLaren has introduced the new 600 LT, which is developed from the 570S Coupe but features a sporty design, optimized aerodynamics, reduced weight and a more powerful engine. On the outside, the McLaren 600LT is equipped with an aerodynamic bodykit with a front bumper, a rear diffuser, a body mainly made of ultra-light carbon fiber and a large rear spoiler. McLaren 600LT is the 4th car to have the prefix Longtail (LT) in the name after the 1997 McLaren F1 GTR 'Longtail' racing car, the McLaren 675LT Coupe and Spider versions.​ The car will begin production in October 2018, according to McLaren, this model has a production time that does not last more than 12 months. McLaren has not announced how many cars will be produced. In the UK market, the McLaren 600LT is priced from £185,000 (about VND 5.5 billion), the price includes the "McLaren Road Owner Track Day" racing experience package with McLaren experts. McLaren 600LT will be a direct competitor of Ferrari 488 Pista or Lamborghini Huracán Performante.​

Strength and presence

The new looks McLaren 600LT much sportier than its predecessor. The Singleframe in octagon design and large side air inlets characterize the masculine front-end. Eight vertical bars divide up the radiator grille with its striking frame and reflect the SUV character. The headlights are narrow and run inwards with their wedge shape, all with LED technology. LED Headlights are standard and 600LT Advanced version comes with Matrix LED Headlights. A highlight in the side view is the symmetrical lighting graphics of the headlights and rear lights. The shoulder line connects them from a styling perspective and provides an athletic overall impression with strong muscles over the wheel arches. The contours draw their inspiration from McLaren quattro DNA and make the SUV seem even wider; the color-contrasting wheel arch trims emphasize the offroad look. Supported by a long roof edge spoiler, which also flanks the rear window at the side, the steeply raked D pillars of the body line also create an appearance of forward thrust. LED rear lights with dynamic indicators round up the muscular silhouette.

Large and adaptable: the space concept

The new mcLaren 600LT, which is based on the Volkswagen Group’s modular transverse matrix, has grown in virtually all dimensions and is positioned in the upper compact SUV segment. It is 4,485 millimeters long, making it 97 millimeters longer than its predecessor. In terms of width, it has grown 25 millimeters to 1,856 millimeters, but at 1,585 millimeters is 5 millimeters flatter than the first-generation 600LT. Its wheelbase, which has been stretched 77 millimeters to 2,680 millimeters is much more spacious – knee room, headroom and elbow room are even larger.

Intelligently operated

The operating and display concept of the family SUV has been overhauled from the ground up – Audi has done away with the analog instruments. MMI radio plus digital instrument cluster comes with a 10.25 inch screen diagonal, which the driver operates using the multifunction steering wheel. Customers can upgrade the instrument cluster to access road and engine speed as well as information on music tracks. MMI Touch Screen and Audi virtual cockpit are standard in the 600LT Line version.

Superb road handling

Maneuverable in town, agile on country roads, balanced on the highway – the suspension on the mcLaren 600T offers a wide spread. The sporty character of the predecessor model has been retained, but enhanced with a clear dose of additional comfort. The new SUV takes bumpy roads with ease – thanks also to the longer wheelbase.


McLaren comes with a 1.5 liter petrol engine, combined with front-wheel drive, delivering 110 kW (150 hp) power output. The turbocharged four-cylinder direct injection engine is powerful, refined and efficient. Power is transferred by a fast-shifting seven-speed S tronic.


The mcLaren in Vietnam is fitted with 18 inch cast alloy wheels,5-spoke Y-style, partly polished, and as an option can go up to 20 inch in diameter, the latter from mcLaren Sport GmbH and fitted with size 255/40 tires.

Modern and advanced cockpit

With the focus on putting the customer experience first, mcLaren is sure to have something great for your seat and the 600LT is no exception.

Even more sportiness with special editions

mcLaren customers can customize their car with numerous optional extras. For instance the S line exterior package, which also includes color highlighting for many details, makes for a decidedly sporty look. The sport suspension, 20 inch wheels and Matrix LED headlights are also included in the equipment specification. Exclusive touches on the special edition model include the Audi rings in the Singleframe and the model designation on the rear finished in black. Pulse orange and Chronos gray are available as paint finishes. Highlights in the interior include sport seats with specific contrasting stitching, the flat-bottomed leather steering wheel, the contour/ambient lighting package and aluminum-look trim. Elements of the instrument panel and the door armrests are finished in Alcantara.
